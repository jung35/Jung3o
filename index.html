About

My name is Jung, a 17-year old Korean High school student living in the United States of America, and I am a Web Developer and a Designer. Since I was young, I have always been interested in computers and the internet. From my interest, I have found myself playing with different computer languages and after few years of learning new things, I've decided I want to design and develop websites.

I have self-taught most of the technology related things so far. I would say I am proficient with Web Developing and Designing. In my free time, I enjoy coming up new designs and layouts of random things just so I can just improve on my designing skills, and I enjoy making little web tools for myself so that I can use just a click away, but also so that I can improve by back-end. I am very passionate about the web and I love learning new things at any moment that I know will help me improve on what I do.
